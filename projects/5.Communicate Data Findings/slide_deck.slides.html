GoBike in San Francisco¶

by Ovidiu-Mihai Anicai¶

Plan to visit?¶

If you plan to visit San Francisco sometime soon I have a good news for you!
There is a fantastic bike rental service called Bay Wheels by Lyft in the bay area!
You just need \$2 to rent a bike for 30 minutes or \$15 / month for unlimited 45 minute trips.
The service is easy to use, just get the App, setup you account and find the nearest station.
(few years back the service was called Ford GoBike)

Data for you¶

In this presentation I will show you how I used the Bay Wheels public datasets to help you during your trip to The City by the Bay.
I took a look on over 4.5 milion rides from 2018 and 2019 in order to get a glimpse on all about the routes, how much time riders pedal or what pricing plan is best for you.
In the next slides I will share with you my findings.

Watch out for the busy places and take short trips¶

The busiest stations are the train and ferry stations, Soma Neighborhood and the ones on The Embarcadero St.
Short rides are the bikers favorite during week days and in the weekend they like to pedall more.
Anyway, in order not to pay extra \$3 per 15 minutes you just need to ride less then 30 minutes.

Best pricing offer for you¶

For users there are three main offers

  • Single Ride with \$2 get a 30 minutes ride
  • Access Pass with \$10 get unlimited 30 minute rides for a day
  • Monthly Memership for \$15 get unlimited 45 minute rides for a month

If you plan to ride more than 5 times or two days you should definitely get the Monthly Memership!
In my analysis I found that very few people choose the first two options, many more go as I recomended
